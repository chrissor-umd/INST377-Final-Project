SCORING

When you check your answers, the color of the text will change depending on how correct you were. The colors mean the following:

  • Green answers are correct and score 1 point

  • Red answers are incorrect and count as 1 mistake

  • Blue answers are incorrect, but synonymous with the correct answer. You don't get any points, but it doesn't count as a mistake
